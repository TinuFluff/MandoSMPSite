Rules

  1. Hacking is prohibited. This includes getting and using the world seed.
  2. Duping is not allowed.
  3. Do not create lag machines.
  4. Cussing as well as NSFW is banned.
  5. No using exploits to gain an unfair advantage. This includes texture packs, and unfair skins.
  6. Do not harass people in DMs, this includes DM advertising.
  7. Combat logging is prohibited. This includes using the !gohome command in battles, as well as any other potential loophole options.
  8. Follow the Discord TOS (no doxxing, don't ban evade etc...)
  9. Follow the city rules, when you are within the protected city, they can be found in the discord.
  10. Hate speech (racist, religious, homophobic, etc...) is prohibited.
  11. Alt accounts are not allowed. We will ban any/all alt accounts that attempt to join. We may ban your main account depending on the severity.
  12. If you wish to report a player, you must have evidence. It must be either photo or video, however video is preferred. Photo evidence may not always be accepted.
